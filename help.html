Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is CashCompass?
CashCompass is a financial management app that helps you track expenses, set budgets, and achieve financial goals.
2. How do I connect my bank account?
Navigate to the "Banking" section in the app, choose your bank, and follow the secure authentication process.
3. Can I manually add expenses?
Yes, you can manually add expenses through the "Add Expense" option in the app.
4. How secure is my data?
CashCompass uses advanced encryption to ensure that your data is safe and secure.
5. Can I set spending limits?
Yes, you can set spending limits in different categories using the budgeting feature.
6. Does CashCompass support multiple currencies?
Yes, CashCompass supports multiple currencies and automatically converts them based on the latest rates.
7. How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
8. Can I export my financial data?
Yes, you can export your data as a CSV file from the "Settings" menu.
9. Who do I contact for support?
You can reach out to our support team via the "Help" section in the app or email support@cashcompass.com.
